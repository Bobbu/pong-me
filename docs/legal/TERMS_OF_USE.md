**READ BEFORE FIRST USE:**

- **Do not use the App if you are prone to seizures** or have been diagnosed
  with photosensitive epilepsy without first consulting a physician.
- **Take regular breaks.** We recommend a minimum 10–15 minute break for
  every 30 minutes of use.
- **Do not use while fatigued, under the influence of alcohol or drugs,

  increase the risks of AR use.
- **Children under 13 should not use AR devices.** For users aged 13–17,
  direct adult supervision is required.
- **Stop immediately** if you experience any of the following: seizures,
  loss of awareness, eye strain, involuntary movements, altered or blurred
  vision, dizziness, disorientation, nausea, lightheadedness, excessive
  sweating, or drowsiness.
