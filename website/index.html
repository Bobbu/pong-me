What is Pong Me?

Pong Me is a lovingly retro take on the original Pong. Bold green paddles, a bouncing ball, and that unmistakable CRT glow — all running at 60fps on your Mac or iPhone.

Every pixel, every sound, and every sprite is generated entirely in code. No art assets, no audio files — just pure procedural craftsmanship.

🎮

Classic Gameplay

Player vs AI with first-to-win scoring (configurable 3, 5, or 7 points). The ball speeds up on every paddle hit, keeping each rally tense. Press R or tap PLAY AGAIN to restart instantly.

🎧

Procedural Audio

Every sound is synthesized from square and sine waves at runtime — wall bounces, paddle hits, scoring buzzes, and a winning arpeggio fanfare. No audio files needed, just math.

⚙

Fully Configurable

In-game Settings panel lets you adjust ball speed (Slow / Medium / Fast), AI difficulty (Easy / Medium / Hard), win score, and sound — all without leaving the game.

Play Anywhere

💻 Mac

1920x1080 windowed, resizable. Keyboard controls (W/S to move, 1/2/3 for speed, M for mute, R to reset, Cmd+Q to quit). Mouse clicks for Settings and Help panels.

📱 iPhone

Touch controls — drag the left side of the screen to move your paddle. Landscape-only, safe area aware (works with notch and Dynamic Island). All settings accessible via tap.
